if the moon could talk

it would tell you...

ke mein tumhe chaand keh doun
ye toh mumkin hai

magar loug tumhe
raat bhar dekhein

ye mujhay gawara nahi

kahan se laaun wo lafz
jo tujay sunai dein

duniya dekhay chand ko per

mujhay toh sirf tu dikhai dey

tum mere saath hogi toh
kya kahega zamana

meri yehi ek tamman'ah hai
tera yehi ek bahana hai

tumhare chehre ki chamak
ke samne saada laga

aasmaan mein chaand poora tha
magar aadha laga

har din tera deedar hou
phir chahay dukh hazaar hon

chaar din ki zindagani
aur beet rahe hein din

dou beetay tere milne se phele
aur dou beetay tere bin

sab hasratoon ko meri araam ageya

teray aashiqon mein mera bhi naam ageya

tum bilkul chaand ki tarhan ho

door bhi
noor bhi
aur ghuroor bhi

jisko mili ho tum
mujay uski hatheli dekhni hai

kaisi hoti hai wo lakeer
jo mere haathon mein nahi hai

meri takleef ki waja tum nahi ho.
takleef ki wajah ye hai ke tum nahi ho

tujhay le kar mera khayal nahi badlega

saal badlega

lekin mere dil ka haal nahi badlega

aur phir...

aaj tum ai nahi..
